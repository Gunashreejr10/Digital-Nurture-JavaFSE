Community Bulletin

The city council has announced new plans for the downtown renovation project, including expanded green spaces, a farmers market pavilion, and improved pedestrian pathways connecting the library to the park. Construction is expected to begin next spring and residents are encouraged to attend the upcoming town hall meeting to share feedback on the proposed designs. Local businesses along Main Street have expressed strong support for the changes, citing increased foot traffic during past community events.
